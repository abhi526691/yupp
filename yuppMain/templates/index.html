Yupp is a full range of 100% Compostable and Biodegradable Food Packaging products manufactured with care, love, and pride in India.

We provide customers with the highest quality in sustainable food packaging products, and food service disposables.

Our disposable tableware products are manufactured from renewable resource – Bagasse, the dry fibrous residue left once sugarcane has been pressed for juice.

 

OUR MISSION

To provide a way for people to use environment friendly products made from renewable resources. We believe we can help achieve a significant reduction in the amount of environmental waste by creating plastic-free products made from renewable resources and providing them to the masses, all over the world.

We intend to augment our product line with exciting products such as eco-friendly cutlery, shopping bags, tissue papers and disposables made from other renewable and eco-friendly materials, under the brand name Yupp.

 

WHAT MAKES US SPECIAL

Our products are:

  • Microwave-safe
  • Freezer Friendly
  • Hot-Food Friendly
  • Water & Oil Resistant
  • Marine-Life Friendly
  • 100% Plastic Free
  • 100% Biodegradable
  • 100% Compostable
  • Made with care and pride in INDIA

We specialize in manufacturing deep-drawn products and are currently one of the few manufacturers of 120ml Round Glasses, 500ml Round Containers and 650ml Meal Boxes with Lids. Our Round Plates are strategically sized at 9″ and 11″ for most convenience and best product strength and quality.

Yupp Range of Disposable Tableware

Yupp is a complete range of oil and water resistant disposable tableware for domestic, restaurant and takeaway use:

  • Round Glass – 120ml
  • Round Containers – with and without lids – from 250ml to 500ml
  • Meal Box - with and without lid - 650ml
  • Round Plates – Plain and with 3/4CP – 9” and 11”
  • Leaf Plates – Plain and with 3/4CP

Our focus is on unique deep-drawn takeaway products that are environment friendly, functional, and versatile. Say YES to Eco-Friendly with Yupp.
